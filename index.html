About Bafenyi Care Centre

Bafenyi Care Centre is a non-profit organisation registered with the Department of Social Development and the Companies and Intellectual Property Commission (CIPC) in South Africa.

Our objective is to provide out-of-this-world care services to needy individuals with physical disabilities. We have realized that due to challenging economic factors, individuals with disabilities are often neglected or not properly cared for.

Our program is based on in-house hosting of disabled individuals at our world-class facility that is specially designed to accommodate different types of disabilities without any struggle of movement or limitations in accessing life-fulfilling moments.

Our Core Services

Full Care

Provided to the frailest residents who need special attention on a full-time basis with a dedicated care worker at all times.

Dependent Care

For residents who can perform certain tasks without full-time care but need close supervision from time to time.

Semi-dependent Care

For residents who can perform most duties independently but need assistance with others due to their disability.

Monitoring Care

For residents who only need periodic monitoring based on certain activities affected by their disability.
