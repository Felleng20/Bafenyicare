About Bafenyi Care Centre

Bafenyi Care Centre is a non-profit organisation registered with the Department of Social Development and the Companies and Intellectual Property Commission (CIPC) in South Africa.

Our objective is to provide exceptional care services to individuals with physical disabilities, creating a home-like environment that promotes holistic development and well-being.

We welcome individuals from the age of 18 years, with capacity for 20 residents (16 sharing and 4 single rooms based on needs and preferences).

Our Vision

To be the best, preferred and caring disability centre in the Country.

Our Mission

To achieve our vision through investing in quality employees, best facility and effective well researched programs.

Our Care Services

We offer personalized care services tailored to each resident's needs:

Full Care

For residents who need special attention on a full-time basis with a dedicated care worker.

Dependent Care

For residents who can perform certain tasks but require close supervision from time to time.

Semi-dependent Care

For residents who can perform most duties but need assistance with specific activities.

Monitoring Care

For residents who only need periodic monitoring based on activities affected by their disability.
